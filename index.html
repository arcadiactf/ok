CTF Guidelines

  • Each team must have a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 3 members.
  • Avoid exploiting the platform, DoS attacks, or sharing flags with other teams.
  • Work within the challenge boundaries — no attacks on unintended targets.
  • Split tasks based on skills (Web, Crypto, Forensics, etc.) for maximum efficiency.
  • Maintain notes of steps and tools used — helps with learning and future writeups.
